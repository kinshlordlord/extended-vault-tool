# Extended Vault Bot

> Automated XVS vault yield optimizer for Extended DEX on Starknet. Monitors vault APY in real time, auto-deposits idle margin when yield is favorable, rebalances between vault and active trading capital, and compounds earnings to maximize returns on your Extended balance.

*Last updated: April 2026*

## What is Extended Vault Bot?

Extended DEX on Starknet features the XVS vault - a yield-bearing vault where deposited USDC earns variable APY generated by Extended's protocol fees and liquidity provision. Extended Vault Bot automates the full XVS yield cycle: it monitors vault APY, deposits idle margin when yield exceeds your threshold, withdraws capital when trading opportunities are detected, and compounds earnings continuously.

![preview_extended-vault-bot](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/22a141dd-3492-466a-bdf4-dcfad1d4629b)

Running alongside Extended Perp Trader or Airdrop Bot, the Vault Bot ensures no capital sits idle. Between farming sessions or between trade setups, your USDC is deployed in the XVS vault earning yield. When a trade signal or farming session starts, the bot withdraws exactly the needed amount and returns the rest to vault.

Extended's Q2 2026 airdrop points may also be allocated partly to vault depositors - the bot tracks this separately.

## Vault Strategy Settings

| Setting | Example | Description |
|---------|---------|-------------|
| `min_apy_to_deposit` | 8.0 | Minimum vault APY % to trigger deposit |
| `max_vault_allocation_pct` | 80 | Maximum % of balance to keep in vault |

## Engine Features

* **Real-time APY monitor** - fetches XVS vault APY from Extended API every poll cycle
* **Auto-deposit** - moves idle capital to vault when APY exceeds configured threshold
* **Auto-withdraw** - pulls capital from vault when trading bot signals a new position entry
* **Auto-compound** - reinvests vault earnings to increase deposited principal
* **Rebalancer** - periodically reviews vault vs trading capital split and rebalances
* **Trading reserve** - always preserves configured USD amount outside vault for immediate trading
* **Vault points tracker** - monitors airdrop points earned through vault participation
* **APY history log** - records vault APY over time for yield analysis

## How It Works

1. **Monitor** - fetches XVS vault APY every poll cycle from Extended API
2. **Evaluate** - checks if current APY exceeds minimum threshold and if rebalance is needed
3. **Deposit/Withdraw** - moves capital between wallet and vault based on APY and trading signals
4. **Compound** - reinvests accumulated vault earnings back into the vault principal
5. **Report** - logs cycle result with APY, deposited balance, earnings, and vault points

## Frequently Asked Questions

**What is the XVS vault on Extended?**
XVS is Extended's yield vault. Depositing USDC earns variable APY generated from Extended's protocol activity. APY fluctuates based on vault utilization and protocol volume.

**How does auto-compound work?**
Vault earnings (interest) are periodically claimed and re-deposited into the vault, increasing your principal and earning more yield in subsequent cycles. The bot handles this on its own.

**What is the trading reserve?**
`trading_reserve_usd` ensures a fixed amount remains outside the vault at all times, ready for immediate position entry without waiting for a vault withdrawal to process.

**Can it work alongside the Airdrop Bot?**
Yes. The Vault Bot integrates with Extended Perp Trader and Airdrop Bot - it pulls capital out when they need it and re-deposits it when they finish. Run both from the same config file.

**Do vault deposits earn airdrop points?**
Extended has indicated vault participants may receive a separate points allocation in the Q2 2026 airdrop. The bot tracks vault deposit time and volume for this purpose.

**What if APY drops below my threshold?**
The bot withdraws capital from the vault and holds it in wallet (earning no yield) until APY recovers above your minimum. You can set an alert threshold to be notified when APY spikes.
